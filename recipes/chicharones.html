Crispy Chicharones

Delicious Chicharones

Ingredients:

  • 1 slab of pork belly raw

Steps:

  1. Prick the Skin
  2. Score the Skin
  3. Boat the belly in foil
  4. Cover with Rock Salt. It will extract moister (will not be salty)
  5. Put in the Oven on Convection Bake - 60 min @ 180c / 350F
  6. Remove ALL Salt
  7. Brush the Skin with Vinegar (you wonâ€™t Taste it)
  8. Put in the oven, Broil/Grill, bottom rack (furthest away from the heat), 25 min @ 250c / 480 F
